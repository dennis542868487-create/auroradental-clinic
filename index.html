Welcome to Aurora

Dental care for the whole family

Aurora Dental Clinic is dedicated to providing the highest quality of dental services for your whole family. Our dentists advocate a non-invasive approach whenever possible.

We believe that through oral health education and preventative care, we can help our patients achieve optimal health. Our clinic is like one big family; we have been serving the Richmond community for over 35 years and have watched families grow up in our care. We look forward to meeting you!

What We Offer

A full spectrum of dental services

Our general dentists are knowledgeable and experienced in performing a wide variety of procedures, with up-to-date technology to maximize patient comfort.

Restorative

Tooth-coloured composite fillings and restorations that restore structural integrity and function — no amalgam.

Endodontics

Root canals and pulp capping, guided by in-house CBCT imaging to pinpoint the problem.

Implants

A permanent, natural-feeling solution for missing teeth — including implant-supported dentures.

Orthodontics

Straighten your smile with ClearCorrect invisible aligners or traditional braces.

Periodontics

Care for the gums, tissue and bone that support your teeth, including grafting and surgery.

Cosmetic & More

Teeth whitening, veneers, prosthodontics and therapeutic botulinum injections.

Our Approach

Preventative care, modern technology

We believe the best dentistry is gentle and proactive. By focusing on education and prevention, we help patients avoid bigger problems down the road.

Our clinic is equipped with up-to-date technology — including in-house CBCT imaging — to make diagnosis precise and treatment as comfortable as possible. Please note we currently do not offer sedation dentistry; all procedures are performed under local anesthetic.
